Our Story

At Leo's Glass, Windows, and Doors, our journey began in 2011 in the heart of California, where we envisioned a company that could transform ordinary spaces into spectacular ones with the finest glass, windows, and doors. Our founder, Leonardo Moreno, brought this vision to life with a commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and a passion for detail.

Over the years, we've become industry leaders, recognized for our quality and innovative work in both residential and commercial projects, from energy-efficient windows to custom glass features.

Our growth has paralleled an increased commitment to sustainability and community, focusing on responsible material sourcing and energy-efficient designs, and supporting local initiatives.
